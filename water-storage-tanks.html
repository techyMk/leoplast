Water Storage Tanks

Advanced engineering · Anti-bacterial protection · Hygienic water storage

Advanced System

Leo Plast Advanced Water Storage Tank System

Leo Plast's water storage tanks integrate advanced engineering with high-quality materials to provide durable, hygienic, and secure water storage solutions. With specialized anti-bacterial properties and a user-friendly threaded lid, these tanks are crafted to serve residential, commercial and agricultural needs.

Manufactured in our state-of-the-art facility, we utilize both Blow Moulding and Roto Moulding techniques to create tanks that stand up to varying climates and ensure the safety and purity of stored water.

Key Features

🦠

Anti-Bacterial Protection

Specially designed anti-bacterial layer ensures water remains free from bacteria, algae, and microbial growth, even with prolonged storage — the most hygienic option in the market.

🔒

Unique Threaded Lid

User-friendly threaded lid ensures a secure, tightly sealed closure that protects water quality. Provides ease of access and enhances the tank's sleek appearance.

State-of-the-Art Manufacturing

In-house Blow Moulding plant with capacity to produce over 200,000 tanks per year. Unique in the region — unlimited design possibilities with enhanced strength.

UV Stabilized

Advanced UV stabilizers protect the tank from sun degradation, ensuring lasting color and structural integrity even in harsh outdoor environments.

Food-Grade Materials

100% food-grade, lead-free material ensures complete safety for potable water storage without chemical leaching.

Consistent Wall Thickness

Virtually stress-free corners and consistent wall thickness for superior structural strength and long service life.

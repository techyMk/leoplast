About Leoplast

40 years of trust, innovation, and excellence in plumbing solutions across Tamil Nadu.

Who Are We?

Tamil Nadu's Premier Plumbing Innovator

At Leo Plast, we believe that plumbing solutions should be more than just functional — they should inspire confidence and create lasting value. With nearly 40 years of commitment to excellence, Leo Plast Pipes & Fittings has evolved into one of Tamil Nadu's leading manufacturers, specializing in a comprehensive range of high-quality piping and water storage solutions.

Our extensive product portfolio includes PVC, cPVC, and uPVC (ASTM) pipes and fittings, alongside durable water storage tanks crafted using advanced blow-molding and roto-molding techniques. We also offer HDPE hoses for efficient irrigation and PTMT water taps.

Driven by a passion for quality, our journey starts with sourcing the finest raw materials and culminates in the delivery of reliable, certified products directly to your doorstep — each meticulously crafted with safety and durability in mind.

Our Purpose

Vision & Mission

Our Vision

To become the leading provider of comprehensive plumbing solutions, known for excellence in quality, innovation, and sustainability within the PVC piping and water storage industry. We envision a future where our products define the benchmarks for reliability and environmental stewardship, contributing to the development of safe and efficient infrastructure in every community we serve.

Our Mission

To be a leader in the PVC piping and water storage industry by providing durable, high-quality, and sustainable products that cater to our customers' diverse needs. We are dedicated to innovation, excellence, and responsible manufacturing practices, supporting safer water solutions and infrastructure growth that positively impact communities and the environment.
